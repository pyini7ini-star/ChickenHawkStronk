Account Deletion Instructions

We respect your decision to delete your ChickenHawkStronk account. This guide will help you through the process and explain what happens when you delete your account.

⚠️ Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data, including statistics, profiles, and saved information, will be permanently removed from our servers.

Before You Delete Your Account

Please consider the following before proceeding with account deletion:

  • All your data will be permanently deleted
  • You will not be able to access your account after deletion
  • Associated subscriptions or services will be cancelled
  • Any shared data or collaborations will be affected
  • We cannot restore your account after deletion

How to Delete Your Account

1 Log in to your account

Sign in to ChickenHawkStronk with your email and password.

2 Navigate to Account Settings

Click on your profile icon in the top-right corner and select "Settings" from the dropdown menu.

3 Select Privacy & Security

In the settings menu, click on "Privacy & Security" to access privacy-related options.

4 Click Delete Account

Scroll to the bottom of the Privacy & Security section and click the "Delete Account" button.

5 Confirm Your Password

Enter your password to confirm your identity and proceed with the deletion.

6 Read the Warning

Carefully review the deletion warning and acknowledge that you understand the consequences.

7 Confirm Deletion

Click the final "Confirm Deletion" button to complete the process. You will receive a confirmation email.

Data Deletion

Upon account deletion:

  • Your personal information will be deleted from active systems
  • Your data will be removed from backups within 30 days
  • Associated analytics and logs will be anonymized
  • Third-party integrations will be disconnected
  • Your email address will not be reusable for 90 days

Data We May Retain

For legal, security, and operational purposes, we may retain:

  • Transaction records (as required by law)
  • Anonymized usage data for analytics
  • Account creation and deletion logs
  • Data required for fraud prevention

Manual Deletion Request

If you cannot delete your account through the above steps, you can submit a manual deletion request:

Send an email to deletion@chickenhawkstronk.com with:

  • Your full name
  • Your email address associated with the account
  • Your username
  • A request to delete your account

We will process your request within 30 days and confirm completion via email.

Right to be Forgotten

Depending on your location, you may have the right to be forgotten under applicable data protection laws. If you wish to exercise this right, please contact our Data Protection Officer at dpo@chickenhawkstronk.com.
